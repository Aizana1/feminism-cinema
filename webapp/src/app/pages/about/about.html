Notes & caveats

  • Gender is recorded as it appears in Wikidata; the dataset includes a small number of non-binary and transgender nominees, grouped pragmatically in the binary charts.
  • “Other / non-person” covers films, studios and countries nominated in collective categories, plus names that could not be resolved.
  • Acting categories (Actor / Actress) are gender-segregated by the Academy's own rules, so they are excluded from the craft-category comparisons.
  • The fighting-words analysis only sees transcribed speeches, which under-represent women — itself a downstream effect of the nomination gap.
